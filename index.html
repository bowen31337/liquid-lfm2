The Technology

Minimal Hybrid Backbone

Traditional Transformers scale poorly on edge devices due to KV-cache memory usage. Pure SSMs (like Mamba) struggle with retrieval. LFM2 finds the "Goldilocks" zone via hardware-in-the-loop search.

The Training Pipeline

1. Pre-Training

10-12T Tokens • 20% Multilingual • Hardware-aware

school
science

Innovation: Decoupled Top-K Distillation

Distilling from a larger teacher usually suffers from "support mismatch". LFM2 uses a novel loss function that separates total probability mass from relative probabilities.

Loss = KL_Binary(Mass) + Mass * KL_Tempered(TopK_Distribution)

Mid-Training (Long Context)

Annealing on 1T high-quality tokens with 32K context length. This phase specifically targets long-range dependencies and reasoning.

timer

2. Context Extension

Ramping up to 32,768 tokens.

3. Post-Training

Refining for Chat and Instructions.

3-Stage Alignment

  • SFT: Supervised Fine-Tuning on 5M+ samples.
  • Alignment: Length-Normalized Direct Preference Optimization (DPO).
  • Merging: Averaging weights of best checkpoints.
